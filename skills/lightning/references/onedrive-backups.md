# OneDrive Backup Options (Linux + macOS)

## OneDrive Linux client (abraunegg)

- Community OneDrive client supports **upload-only** and selective sync.
- Suitable for one-way backup of `channel.backup` and LNbits DB dumps.
  Source:
- https://abraunegg.github.io/

## OneDrive sync options catalog (includes rclone)

- Microsoft-maintained list of compatible OneDrive clients.
- rclone is listed as a supported sync client option.
  Source:
- https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/blog/onedriveblog/onedrive-open-source-code-and-community-driven-development/1350231
